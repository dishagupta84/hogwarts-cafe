About The Hogwarts Cafe


The Hogwarts Cafe at Platform 9¾ is a magical destination for all wizarding and Muggle visitors alike. We serve delicious treats inspired by the wizarding world — from Butterbeer to Pumpkin Pasties. Our café ambience brings you closer to the Hogwarts experience with mystical decor and wizard-inspired delicacies.

Whether you're arriving from Diagon Alley or just stepping out of the Muggle world, we welcome you to enjoy moments of magic, friendship, and unforgettable flavors at our café.
